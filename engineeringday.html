National Engineers Day 2024 Organized by UTD,CSVTU

National Engineers Day 2024, organized by CSVTU UTD, anticipates a dynamic event focused on emerging technologies, collaboration, and innovation. The occasion aims to bring together engineers, students, and industry leaders for insightful seminars and workshops, providing a glimpse into the future of engineering. It is poised to inspire and foster a culture of innovation, emphasizing the crucial role engineers play in shaping the evolving technological landscape.
